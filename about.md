    <h1>About this site</h1>
    <p>This site documents my astrophotography workflow, from capture sessions in Santa Fe to post-processing experiments on galaxies and nebulae.</p>
    <p>Most images here were captured with a Celestron Origin setup. I use each post to preserve the key metadata shown in the image overlays: target, exposure, date, time, and location.</p>
    <p>The gallery is designed as a clean dark portfolio with post pages that highlight both the image and the capture details.</p>
